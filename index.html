About

The company Auto Import Baku was founded in 2018. The main direction of our company's activities is the import of cars from various countries around the world (mostly from the USA, Canada, the Republic of Korea, China) to the Republic of Azerbaijan, as well as to other countries. Auto Import Baku provides the purchase and insured delivery of cars from all major auctions in the USA and Canada (Copart, Iaai, Manheim), as well as from showrooms, sales centers, and dealer networks.

Due to the diversity of our activities, we have the opportunity to purchase cars not only from the USA but also from domestic auctions in Korea.

n accordance with the requirements of the new era, Auto Import Baku provides the purchase and insured delivery of new hybrid, electric, and plug-in hybrid cars from various countries, mainly from China. Since 2018, our company has successfully imported more than 1600 cars to Azerbaijan. Unlike many other companies in this industry in Azerbaijan, we offer engine and transmission insurance for the cars we purchase. Unlike some companies, we enable the client to participate in the auction in person.

Unlike some companies, we give the client the opportunity to personally take part in the auction. Shipping costs are minimal and we do not charge extra. Prices include port taxes. The cost of our services is adjusted depending on the quality of the services we provide.

We treat the client according to the principle from A to Z, that is, we help and take responsibility from the purchase of the car to its complete transfer and even repair.We conclude a full official agreement with the client, all other payments except for the service fee are made through the bank, the car is imported directly into the country in the name of the client.

We offer the customer real prices, that is, we help buyers set the maximum price at the auction, without creating conditions for the customer to incur additional costs.Our main goal is to seriously influence the price of the car in the local market.
